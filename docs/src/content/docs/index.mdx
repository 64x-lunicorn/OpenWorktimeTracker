## Why OpenWorktimeTracker?

No Dock icon. No manual start. Just open your Mac and your workday begins.

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Auto-Start" icon="rocket">
    Launches at login and starts tracking immediately. No buttons to press, no
    apps to open.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Smart Day Detection" icon="puzzle">
    Automatically detects new workdays -- overnight idle, sleep/wake, and app
    restarts are handled seamlessly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="ArbZG-Compliant Breaks" icon="approve-check-circle">
    Automatic break calculation per German labor law (ArbZG SS4). Fully
    configurable thresholds.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Idle Detection" icon="warning">
    Detects inactivity and asks: "Was that a meeting or a break?" You decide,
    the app adjusts.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Live Menu Bar" icon="bars">
    Shows net work time directly in the menu bar, color-coded by your configured
    thresholds.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Daily JSON Logs" icon="document">
    Transparent, human-readable, exportable. One JSON file per day, plus CSV
    export.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Auto-Updates" icon="refresh">
    Seamless updates via Sparkle and GitHub Releases. Always up to date.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Ethereal Design" icon="star">
    Glassmorphism, tonal depth, adaptive light and dark mode. Beautiful by
    default.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
